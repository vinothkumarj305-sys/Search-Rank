About Vinoth Kumar

I'm Vinoth Kumar, an SEO + AI Search Strategist who helps brands grow organic visibility in both traditional search and AI answer engines — for SaaS, B2B, and growth-focused companies worldwide.

My Story

I started in SEO over eight years ago, obsessed with understanding how search engines crawl, index, and rank content. Today, that obsession extends to a new frontier: AI search — how large language models retrieve, synthesize, and cite sources when answering user queries.

I've helped dozens of brands move from invisible to authoritative — fixing technical debt, building topical authority, and structuring content so it ranks on Google and gets recommended by AI tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews.

My Methodology

Every engagement follows a data-driven, transparent process:

  • Audit first — technical SEO, content gaps, and AI visibility baseline
  • Prioritize impact — focus on changes that move rankings and citations
  • Build entity authority — structured data, E-E-A-T signals, and clear brand positioning
  • Measure everything — track organic traffic, rankings, and AI mentions over time

Who I Work With

I partner with SaaS companies, B2B brands, agencies, and in-house marketing teams who want a strategist — not just a checklist. If you care about measurable growth and staying ahead of how search is evolving, we're a good fit.
